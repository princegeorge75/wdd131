Brief About

Career Institute is the only IATA affiliated institute Uganda, providing a holistic approach to learning with the best facilities and environment for students to enhance their aviation future.

Brief History

Our history goes back to the year 1973 when the school was founded by Prof. George Albert, who was fond of aeronautics.

Achievement

We have achieved numerous milestones in skilling and empowering the rising generations with practical knowledge and experience. Winning the Best Aviation Institute, African Region 7 times and also becoming the people's choice school from 2016 up to date.
